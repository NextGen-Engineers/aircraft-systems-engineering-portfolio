# Stakeholder Needs

## Stakeholder 1: Pilot

- Needs accurate information about available fuel throughout the flight.
- Needs timely alerts when fuel quantity becomes critically low.

## Stakeholder 2: Maintenance Technician

- Needs the system to report faults that simplify troubleshooting.
- Needs maintenance information that supports efficient repairs.
  
## Stakeholder 3: Airline Operator

- Needs reliable fuel management to support safe and efficient operations.
- Needs fuel usage information that supprts efficient flight planing and operational decision making.

## Stakeholder 4: Systems Engineer

- Needs clear, verifiable requirements for software development.
- Needs system behavior that can be tested and validated.

## Stakeholder 5: Aviation Regulator

- Needs evidence that the system supports safe aircraft operation.
- Needs documentation demonstrating compliance with applicable safety requirements.
